Client-focused Guided Trips

At Robber's Roost Outfitters, safety and our clients come first. Our guides are ACA accredited and professionally trained ensuring the safety of our clients througout the entire trip.

Great Locations

Our trips are focused on the hidden gems off the beaten track where people find it difficult to near impossible to access in a rental car. These areas include the San Rafael Swell, Robber's Roost, The Maze district of Canyonlands National Park and North Wash areas of Southern Utah.

Awesome Activities

We offer adventurous hiking and canyoneering day trips from 3 to 10 hours in length as well as multi-day backpacking trips from 2-4 days in length. All gear and food included. Just bring your sense of adventure (and your clothes)!

Canyoneering Courses

Learn the basics of the sport of canyoneering such as rappelling techniques, down-climbing and chimneying. Ready to learn advanced techniques? Our advanced canyoneering course teaches you to build and evaluate anchors, pothole escape and partner-assisted down-climbing.
